8. How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete everything: Uninstall the Extension — all data is automatically and permanently removed.
  • Delete specific data: Open the Extension settings and clear individual fields.
  • Alternative: Go to chrome://extensions, find EtsyCraft AI, click “Details,” then “Clear data.”
  • Payment data: Contact ExtensionPay/Stripe or email support@ventossoft.com.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Access (Art. 15): All data is stored locally and accessible to you at any time.
  • Rectification (Art. 16): Update settings and API keys through the Extension.
  • Erasure (Art. 17): Uninstall the Extension to delete all data.
  • Restrict Processing (Art. 18): Stop using specific features while keeping stored data.
  • Data Portability (Art. 20): Export via Chrome DevTools.
  • Object (Art. 21): Stop all processing by uninstalling.
  • Complaint: Contact CNPD (Portugal) or your local data protection authority.
